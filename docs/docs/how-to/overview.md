# Overview

Sproot was designed to be super flexible to meet whatever inventive scenarios you have in mind. But the funny thing about flexible systems is that often times the more capable it is, the more complicated it becomes to actually leverage its features. And while good design should be self-explanatory, sometimes it's really hard to find a way to make something that makes sense to everyone.

At any rate, these docs are here to hopefully make the capabilities of Sproot make sense to more people. Within them, you'll find detailed descriptions of Sproot's features and some how to's for leveraging these features to meet the inventive scenarios you have in mind.
